MENU

Our Restaurant Best Food

  • Soupe à l’oignon

    The soup’s unique flavor comes from the caramelization of the onions, which often have brandy or sherry added during the slow-cooking process.

  • Ratatouille

    From Provence, the dish sees vegetables shallow-fried and then layered in a casserole dish before being baked in an oven.

  • Tarte Tatin

    Dark and sticky caramel, sweet apples and crisp pastry combine to make this heavenly French dessert.

  • Macarons

    Macarons are delicate and colorful French pastries that are made with almond flour, egg whites, and sugar. They have a crisp shell and a chewy center, and are filled with various flavors of buttercream, ganache, or jam.

  • Tarte Tropézienne

    Tarte Tropézienne is a decadent pastry that is made with a brioche dough, filled with a rich mixture of crème pâtissière and crème au beurre. It is a popular dessert, and is often enjoyed as a treat on the French Riviera.

  • Café au lait

    Café au lait is a classic French coffee drink that is made by combining equal parts coffee and steamed milk. It is a popular choice for breakfast and can be enjoyed hot or cold.
